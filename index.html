Trade Action Framework

Hedge / Risk Guidance

Open stock risk: if physical cotton/comber held and ICE + Indian spot both weaken → protect by selling ICE / reducing inventory.
Forward-sale risk: if sold forward and raw cotton tightens → protect by buying ICE / call option.
FX risk: import/export exposure → USD/INR forward or option.
